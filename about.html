Me, in a nutshell

Product Designer

I'm a passionate Product Designer with background in Art Direction & Training Management for 15 years. I seek to achieve shared objectives by bringing clarity to complex problems through cross-functional collaboration. I help companies thrive by aligning customer needs with critical business goals and translating them into workable solutions.

Art Director

During my many years as an Art Director I already had the chance to work with a wide range of small to global companies. I developed solution strategies for a great variety of problems and learned how to implement them in perfect visual style. My motivation and empathy, broad knowledge and creative conceptual strength have always helped me to successfully lead my clients to their goals [worked for BAUHAUS, Hussel, Müller, C&A, NürnbergMesse, Greiff, Prinovis, BAUR, BonPrix and many more...]

Training Manager

Besides products and businesses, I also love to support real people in their growing process. Therefore, I could additionally gain years of expertise and know-how as a Training Manager and IHK examiner.

Fine Art Designer

Inspired by my love for the graphic-technical design process as well as a strong passion for holistic spatial aesthetics and the highest level of detail perfectionism, I create artworks that excellently combine my enthusiasm for these disciplines.
