Responsive Health WebApp »Painbuddies«

Client: Solo Project

Description: Comprehensive development of a digital health portal for people with stress-related chronic pain as well as an accompanying app that supports patients in their recovery process during and after multimodal pain therapy.

Responsibilities: Design thinking process, user-centerd design, emotional and visual design, competitive analysis, generative and evaluative research, user personas and business requirements, mobile-first and responsive design, information architecture and user flows, iterative wireframing and prototyping, usability and preference testing, design documentation and presentation

B2B2C Marketing and Branding

Client: Greiff

Description: In the course of redesigning the two different fashion lines „Corporate Wear“ and „Gastro Moda“ (main sales media), this project required comprehensive brand development through progressive changes in Corporate Design (logo, slogans, typography, graphic elements, etc.), a new, confident photo approach, as well as focus on the topics of unique selling points, information architecture, functionality, video and webshop.

Responsibilities: Art Direction of a design team, cooperation with cross-functional departments (text/editorial, photography, interactive) and print service providers, brand / competitive / target group analysis, further development of the Corporate Design, further development of formats and page flows (IA), elaboration of new functionalities as a distribution tool, creation of two new design approaches and photo concepts, new and redesign of fundamental contents and layouts, extensive design documentation and presentation
